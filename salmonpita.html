Smoked Salmon Flatbread










Ingredients: Skinless salmon, basil, pita pockets, yoghurt, onion, tomatoes, salt, pepper, lemon and olive oil

slice the salmon and sprinkle it with salt

place Salmon on a pan and grill it.

remove the salmon from the pan and lightly grill the the pita.

Assemble the sandwhiches with salmon, onions, tomatoes, yoghurt, basil and add pepper to taste.
