Pork and Potatoe casserole

Ingredients: 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, 6 boneless pork chops, 1 can condensed mushroom soup, 1 cup milk, 4 potatoes thinly sliced, 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Heat oil in a large skilled over medium high-heat. Place pork chops in the skillet.

In a medium bowl, combine the soup and the milk. Arrange the potatoes and onions in a baking dish. Place the browned chops over the potatoes and onions. Then pour the soup over the dish. Bake for 30 minutes.
