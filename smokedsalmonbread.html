Smoked Salmon Flatbread










Ingredients: Shallots, Salmon, salt and pepper, Lime juice, flatbread, goat cheese

Thinly slice shallots and fry them in olive oil till they are golden brown

Pre-heat your oven to 300F. Spread goat cheese, shallots, Bake for 10 minutes.

Add the salmon, sprinkle black pepper, basil (option), microgreens (optional) and then bake for 5 minutes

Slice and Serve! add lime juice for extra flavor
